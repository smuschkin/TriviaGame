Which is NOT a benefit of fiber?

Fiber helps regulate the body’s use of sugars. Fiber helps promote satiety. Fiber helps enhance neuron function. Fiber helps stabilize blood sugar levels.

What is a soluble fiber and what does it do?

Fiber that does not dissolve in water, helping promote regular bowel movements by moving food through digestive system. Fiber that dissolves in water, helping lower glucose and cholesterol levels. Fiber that does not dissolve in fat, helping absorb vitamins. Fiber that dissolves in fat, helping eliminate adipose cells.

What is an insoluble fiber and what does it do?

Fiber that does not dissolve in water, helping promote regular bowel movements by moving food through digestive system. Fiber that dissolves in water, helping lower glucose and cholesterol levels. Fiber that does not dissolve in fat, helping absorb vitamins. Fiber that dissolves in fat, helping eliminate adipose cells.

What are good sources of soluble fibers?

Fish, steak, chicken and eggs Olive oil, coconut oil, avocado and sunflower seed oil Whole grains, brown rice, legumes, carrots, and tomatoes Oatmeal, nuts, beans, apples and blueberries

What are good sources of insoluble fibers?

Fish, steak, chicken and eggs Olive oil, coconut oil, avocado and sunflower seed oil Whole grains, brown rice, legumes, carrots, and tomatoes Oatmeal, nuts, beans, apples and blueberries

Which is NOT a good way to help increase fiber in the diet?

Eat whole fruits instead of drinking fruit juices. Replace whole grains and brown rice with white rice, bread and pasta. Substitute beans or legumes for meat 2-3x/week. Snack on vegetables instead of chips, crackers and candy.

Which is NOT a fact about fiber?

Low fiber diets that are high in foods with high glycemic indexes increase the risk of type 2 diabetes. Fiber in wheat and oat bran is more effective than fiber in fruits and vegetables to help relieve constipation. Women who eat more high-fiber foods during adolescence and adulthood have significantly lower risk of breast cancer. A high fiber diet raises the risk of heart disease by increasing cholesterol.
